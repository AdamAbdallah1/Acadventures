Culture & Safety Tips

Small habits can change your whole experience abroad. Blend in better, stay safer, and travel with fewer stressful moments.

Learn the Magic Five

Hello, Please, Thank You, Sorry, and “Do you speak English?” go a long way in any country.

The Two-Wallet Rule

Carry one wallet for daily use and keep a backup card and cash stored separately.

Stay Alert, Not Nervous

Walk confidently, avoid displaying valuables, and always know your route before moving at night.

PRO TIP

The Incognito Flight Search Trick

When comparing flights repeatedly, use private browsing to keep your search clean and make comparison easier without clutter from previous sessions.
